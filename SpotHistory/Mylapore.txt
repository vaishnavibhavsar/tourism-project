SPOT NAME: MYLAPORE

ADDRESS:  Mylapore H.O, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India (IN), Pin Code:-600004

SPECIALITY: Mylapore is home to hundreds of temples, churches and mosques.
	    
HISTORY: Mylapore ante-dates British rule by several centuries. It was historically known as Vedapuri. 
	 As the available historical and archaeological evidence show, it could well be the oldest part of Chennai, 
	 with written records of early settlements going back to the first century BCE.
