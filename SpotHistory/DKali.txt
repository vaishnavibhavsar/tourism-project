SPOT NAME:DAKSHINESHWAR KALI TEMPLE,KOLKATA

ADDRESS:Park Street, Kolkata

SPECIALITY:Hindu navaratna temple

HISTORY:
	*The temple is dedicated to Bhavatarani, an aspect of Goddess Kali and 
	'the savior of the universe'. One of the oldest and most important temples
	 to have ever been built, it receives pilgrims from all over the world.
	*The temple was founded by Rani Rashmoni, who lost her husband and was 
	 left with a vast amount of wealth. 
	*The idea of building this temple came to her in a dream and this was the 
	 last thing she did before going on a pilgrimage to Varanasi.
