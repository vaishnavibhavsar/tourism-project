SPOT NAME:BELUR MATH, KOLKATA

ADDRESS:Belur, Howrah, West Bengal 711202

SPECIALITY: Motifs as a symbol of unity of all religions.

HISTORY:
	*Belur Math is located on the banks of the river Hooghly and is the headquarters 
	 of the Ramakrishna Math. You can see Swami Vivekananda's room here and the old shrine.
	*This temple is the heart of the Ramakrishna Movement. 
	*A direct monastic disciple of Ramakrishna. Sri Ramakrishna Temple was consecrated on 14 January, 
	 the Makar Sankranti Day in 1938.
