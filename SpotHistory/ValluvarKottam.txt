SPOT NAME: VALLUVAR KOTTAM

ADDRESS: 34, Valluvar Kottam High Road, Nungambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, 600034 India

SPECIALITY: The monument consists of a decorative arch, an auditorium that can accommodate around 3,500 people, 
            

HISTORY:   The construction of the Valluvar Kottam was conceived and executed by the then Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu M. Karunanidhi during the 1970s.
           It was designed by South Indian traditional architect V. Ganapati Sthapati, who is also the architect of the Thiruvalluvar Statue at Kanyakumari.
           It was inaugurated in April 1976 by the then President of India Fakhruddin Ali Ahmed.
	   The hallmark of the monument is the 39-meter-high (128 feet) stone car, a replica of the famed temple chariot of Thiruvarur.
