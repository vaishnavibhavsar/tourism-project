SPOT NAME:JORASANKO THAKUR BARI, KOLKATA

ADDRESS:Singhi Bagan, Jorasanko, Kolkata, West Bengal 700007

SPECIALITY: Important centres of social reform, and Bengali literature.(Rabindranath Tagore)

HISTORY:
	*Ancestral home of late Tagore and the house has three separate galleries 
	 filled with paintings, personal items of the Tagore family, including his books and manuscripts. 
	*The house is surrounded by a lush garden and is a peaceful spot in the 
	 middle of the city.
