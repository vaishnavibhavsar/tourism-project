SPOT NAME:KALIGHAT KALI TEMPLE, KOLKATA

ADDRESS:Anami Sangha, Kalighat, Kolkata, West Bengal 700026

SPECIALITY:One of the 51 Shakti Peethas of India

HISTORY:
	*Kalighat is regarded as one of the 51 Shakti Peethas of India, where the various parts 
	 of Sati's body are said to have fallen, in the course of Shiva's Rudra Tandava. 
	*Kalighat represents the site where the toes of the right foot of Dakshayani or Sati fell.
