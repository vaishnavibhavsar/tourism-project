SPOT NAME: SHRI ASHTALAKSHMI TEMPLE

ADDRESS: 6/21 paindi Amman Kovil, Elliots Beach, Besant Nagar, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600090

SPECIALITY: The Ashtalakshmi Kovil is a Hindu temple, which lies on the shorelines near the Elliot's beach, in Chennai, India.
	    
HISTORY: The temple was constructed on the wishes of Sri Chandrashekarendra Saraswati swamigal of Kanchi Mutt. 
	 Sri Mukkur Srinivasa Varadhachariar was the Mahatma instrumental behind fulfilling the wishes of Kanchi Periyava. 
	 The foundation was laid in January 1974 by public participation.
